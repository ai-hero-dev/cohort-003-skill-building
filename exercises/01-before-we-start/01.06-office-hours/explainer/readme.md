## Watching Office Hours

All sessions are recorded and available as YouTube VODs. **This is absolutely not mandatory viewing.** It's mostly for vibes and having a bit of fun during the course.

If you want to walk through something in depth, office hours is the perfect time. Any interesting topics that come up usually get recorded as standalone explainers in the course.
